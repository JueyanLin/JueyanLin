Yumeiro Patissiere

A sweet world


This is a website about the Japanese anime Yumeiro Patissiere.
I created this site out of my love for this manga.
This anime that I watched as a child brought back many fond memories of baking and the healing power of cake.
I hope that more people can feel the beauty and happiness that desserts bring.
